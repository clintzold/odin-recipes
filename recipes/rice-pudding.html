Comforting Rice Pudding Recipe

There is nothing like coming home from long, cold day in winter and dipping a spoon in a warm bowl of rice pudding.

With a few simple ingredients and about 30 minutes of your time, comforting rice pudding can be yours any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup of raisins
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil
  2. Add rice, raisins, cinnamon and salt and stir lightly. Cover pot with lid
  3. Remove lid once water returns to boil and allow to cook until water is level with rice
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and pour in condensed milk
  5. Stir frequently to avoid burning for 5 minutes
  6. Remove from stove, set aside to cool.
  7. Pudding will thicken as it cools. Enjoy while warm or cold from the fridge the next day!
