Odin's Famous Burger

It's a hot, sunny day; there's a cold beer in your hand and smoke wafting from the bbq grill while a plate full of burgers cool on the picnic table in front of you. Your friends smile and prepare the condiments - you are the KING!

With Odin's famous burger recipe, you can own moments like these all summer long. Thick and juicy, sizzling with flavour - these burgers are sure to please the fussiest of friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound medium ground beef
  • 1 white onice(sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes(sliced)
  • 4 burger buns
  • Condiments of your choosing
  • Beer(Optional)
  1. Heat grill
  2. Form 4 equal beef patties by hand
  3. Put patties on grill
  4. Close lid of bbq
  5. Drink beer for 3-5 minutes while the burgers sizzle
  6. Flip burgers and repeat the process
  7. Stack patties on a plate
  8. Build burgers with onion, tomatoes and condiments of your choice
  9. Enjoy!
