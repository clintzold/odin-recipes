A Delicious Home-Made Lasagna Recipe

Have you ever craved a succulent slice of lasagna but Nonna's already locked away in a retirement home? Look no further!

Odin's homemade lasagna recipe hits every spot you've ever had! With fresh tomatoes, beef, onions and loaded with authentic Italian mozzarella cheese - it's sure to leave you drooling for more.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until soft. Strain and set aside.
  2. Crush the tomatoes into a sauce
  3. Finely chop the onion and garlic
  4. Pan fry ground beef, onions & garlic until lightly browned
  5. Lightly grease pan and lay noodles flat, covering the bottom
  6. Slice mozzarella into 1/4" slices
  7. Layer beef mixture and tomato sauce on top of noodles and cover with cheese slices
  8. Repeat previous steps until number of layers desired is achieved
